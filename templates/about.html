About SkinCareAI

Our AI-powered Skin Cancer Triage System helps detect skin cancer early, assisting doctors and patients with timely diagnosis.

Our Mission

To provide early detection tools for skin cancer, improving survival rates and patient awareness.

How It Works

Users can upload images of skin lesions, and our AI model analyzes the image to predict the risk of cancer.

Why SkinCareAI?

Accurate, fast, and easy-to-use system that empowers healthcare professionals and patients to take preventive action.
